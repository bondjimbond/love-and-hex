1. Dojo the chicken-legged house strolls happily along a pretty forest path. Alond, the deposed evil king, struggles behind the house, carrying a large wooden chest on his back.


2. Dojo leaps across a stream on rocks. Alond has fallen into the stream, holding the chest above his head.


3. Dojo climbs up a mountain, kicking rocks up in its wake. Alond struggles behind, dragging the chest.


4. On top of the mountain, Alond is collapsed under the chest. "I can't... go... any further..."

From inside Dojo, a voice: "Whoa, dojo."


5. Kayra easily lifts the chest off of Alond's back. Singing Cricket helps him up. He's still wearing his false moustache.

Singing Cricket: "It's okay, buddy. You did great."


6. Kayra holds the chest in one hand, says some magic words, and the chest shrinks.


7. Singing Cricket guides a limping Alond toward the house.

Singing Cricket: "Let's get you inside."

Alond, wide-eyed, points at Kayra, who is putting the now-tiny chest into her pocket. "Buh- buh, bu-buh--"
