1. A view through Tamilda's periscope into the window. We see Kayra sleeping in her room, on a man's shoulder. The man is Singing Cricket, but his face is not visible.

Tamilda, in caption: "That's her! Looks like she found another victim."


2. Tamilda taking careful aim with her bow and arrow. She thinks, "I hope it's not too late for him..."


3. The camera follows the arrow on its trajectory toward Kayra's head. The couple continue to sleep.


4. Singing Cricket stirs. Still asleep, with a "snork" and a bit of drool coming down the corner of his mouth, he instinctively and unconsciously snatches the arrow from the air centimetres from sleeping Kayra's head.


5. Still asleep, he casually tosses the arrow back out the window.


6. Tamilda ducks with an "Ack!" as the arrow flies just over her head and embeds itself in a tree with a THWAP.


7. Looking serious, Tamilda starts moving toward the house, drawing her sword. "Wait, I recognize those moves..."
